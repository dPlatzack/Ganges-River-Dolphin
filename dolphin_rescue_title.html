Ganges Dolphin Rescue

By: Lucas Breadmore, Alex Birse, David Platzack, Josie Clark.

This is a choose-your-own adventure interactive game that highlights the dangers that the Ganges River Dolphin face and how you can help to save this threatened species. You have been tasked with protecting the lives of the Ganges River Dolphins in South Asia.

Good luck.
